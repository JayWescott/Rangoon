Established in 2017 by Myanmar native, Chef Alex Saw (Country Cat, Bollywood Theater), and Portland native, Chef Nick Sherbo (Bollywood Theater, Matt's BBQ), Rangoon Bistro is an emerging pop-up restaurant and regular vendor at the Portland Farmers Market - King Market.

The cuisine of Myanmar is influenced by the five countries with whom it shares a border, India, China, Laos, Thailand, and Bangladesh, but also retains distinctive ingredients and dishes singularly Burmese.
Our aim is to broaden the exposure of burmese food by serving classic dishes like Lahpet Thoke, and Mohinga while simultaneously recognizing the abundance of the pacific northwest and utilizing it’s bounty. We trust our experience as chefs with diverse cooking backgrounds to handle the food ways of Myanmar with reverence but without a ridged sense of authenticity. Our ultimate goal is to serve honest delicious food that can be enjoyed amongst friends and families.
